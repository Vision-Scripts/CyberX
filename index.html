About Us

CyberX , the Cyber Security Club of Delhi Public School R.K. Puram , was established in 2023 to equip students with essential skills in cybersecurity and ethical hacking. Our mission is to create a tech-savvy community prepared to tackle the challenges of the digital world.

Our programs are hands-on and engaging, ensuring practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge. We organize regular workshops, seminars, and competitions, collaborating with industry experts and educational institutions to provide up-to-date training.
